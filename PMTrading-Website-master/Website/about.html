What we do ?

P.M Trading Corporation is a vibrant and pioneering business house that offers quality bearings solutions of international standards. Driven by an extraordinary workforce, our superior customer support help us to meet all requirements of our customers with a complete series of Standard duty 200, medium duty x00, heavy duty 300 series inserts & complete units, bearings, stainless steel bearings & bearing unit , thermoplastic bearing & Bearing units , agriculture bearings , zinc plated bearings & Special Bearings.

Why Choose Us?

As a premier bearing importer & supplier we at pmtc aim to provide the best parts for specific applications instead of merely offering basic solutions. However that process involves finding and partnering with the best bearing manufactures to supply around the world to supply quality, specialized bearings. Each bearing manufacture has certain strengths for varying industries such as NBR. We also deal in all types of bearings Needle, Tapper Roller , Angular Contact bearings , Spherical bearings ,Rod End Bearings & Also Deep groove bearings
